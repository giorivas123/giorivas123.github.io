UI/UX App Concept Motion

MusicHub

A modern music app concept focused on discovery, collection, and a premium listening experience.

Project Progress

  • Goal: To prototype a modern music app using Figma that helps users discover and connect with smaller artists, view upcoming performances, and explore music festivals and events.
  • Role: As the UI/UX Designer, I designed the interface and created a fully interactive prototype, using Figma’s prototyping features to simulate real app functionality.
  • Process: The process began with sketching initial concepts, followed by designing and prototyping the app entirely in Figma to refine layout, navigation, and user flow.
  • Focus: The primary focus was highlighting smaller artists while creating meaningful connections between users, artists, and local music events.
  • Outcome: The final result was a fully functioning Figma prototype, providing a strong foundation for future development and demonstrating a complete, user-centered app experience.

Product Design Mobile Prototype

SIPS

A local beverage discovery app designed to help users find new drinks, spots, and favorites faster.

Project Progress

  • Goal: To create a user-friendly app that connects users with local drink shops, supporting small businesses instead of corporate companies.
  • Role: As the UI/UX Designer, I was responsible for sketching and designing the interface in Figma, as well as fully coding the app using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Process: The process involved extensive creative brainstorming with my group members (SIPS), collaborating on ideas, and developing a strategic game plan to bring the app to life.
  • Focus: My primary focus was ensuring the app was intuitive, visually appealing, and easy for users to navigate and enjoy.
  • Outcome: Upon completion, our project was hand-selected to be showcased at a seminar. The app received strong interest from both users and fellow designers, with many expressing enthusiasm for it becoming a real, fully launched product.
