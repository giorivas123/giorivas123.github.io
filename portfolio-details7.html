SIPS Summary

**SIPS** is a mobile app designed to help users discover local beverage shops in their area. Whether you're looking for a cozy coffee shop, a trendy bubble tea spot, or a refreshing juice bar, SIPS provides curated recommendations based on location. In addition to finding great drink spots, users can share their experiences by posting content about their recent visits, rating new finds, and engaging with a community of fellow beverage enthusiasts.
